# BLAST Sequence Analysis

BLAST (Basic Local Alignment Search Tool) is a bioinformatics tool used to compare biological sequences and identify regions of similarity between genes across different species.

## Objective

To investigate the evolutionary conservation of the TP53 gene.

## Method

The TP53 gene sequence was analyzed using the NCBI BLAST tool to compare sequence similarity across different organisms.

## Observations

Significant sequence similarity was observed between human TP53 and TP53 genes in other mammals.

## Interpretation

The high conservation of TP53 across species indicates that this gene performs essential biological functions that have been preserved throughout evolution.

Evolutionary conservation is often a strong indicator of biological importance.
